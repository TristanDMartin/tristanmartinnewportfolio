What began as a side project has evolved into an exciting venture in the development of personal websites for Twitch streamers. The goal was to create a dedicated online space where streamers could showcase their content and provide fans with easy access to all their social links in one convenient area. By building these personalized websites, I aimed to enhance the streamers' online presence, strengthen their connection with fans, and provide a centralized hub for their community to engage and explore their content across various platforms.
